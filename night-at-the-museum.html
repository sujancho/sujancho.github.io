What is it

Night at the Museum: Hidden Treasures is a spin-off of Pocket Gem’s Secret Passages where fans can continue exploring the world of the Night at the Museum franchise through a casual game experience.

Why I joined

Night at the Museum was Pocket Gem’s first experiment with a franchised title. The shortened timeline, small team, and a high level of autonomy was the perfect environment for me to test and hone my design skills.

This was also a great opportunity for me to develop my visual design process.

As senior designer I was responsible for executing on all aspects of the game’s interface.

What I accomplished

  • Work with the Art Director to concept and product the visual design of the game interface
  • Design, wireframe, and implement UX improvements to key user flows
  • Design and execution was completed on an accelerated timeline.
