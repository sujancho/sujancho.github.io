Key Skills

product vision, strategy, cross-disciplinary collaboration, user research, usability testing, storyboarding, prototyping, design operations, mentorship

What is it

Wild Beyond is a free-to-play real time strategy (RTS) game with the visual quality and strategic gameplay depth of a console game in a mobile-first package.

Pocket Gems aimed to create their next successful franchise by satisfying the wants of Console & PC Gamers 17-35 looking for deep gameplay on the go.

Featured “Game of the Day” on Apple App Store (11/24/2018)

Why I joined

Wild Beyond represented a new direction for Pocket Gems, and I was excited to work on a game that I was wanted to play myself.

It was also a huge opportunity for personal growth, to be on the ground floor and contribute to a product development cycle from concept to launch.

As UX lead, I grew and managed the UX team responsible for the game’s user experience over a 4 year development cycle and worldwide launch.

Major Challenges

  • As a team we struggled between prioritizing between 2 audiences; balancing depth and strategy for core gamers versus learnability and usability with mainstream mobile audience
  • A Major competitor launched when we were mid-development which disrupted our product strategy and led to a major internal game design pivot.
  • We worked with a new in-house 3D engine that was being developed in tandem with our game. Game rendering required learning and managing multiple tech stacks (iOS native, Cocos2D, Cocos3D, HTML/CSS/Angular).

What I accomplished

  • Grew the design team as project scaled; including hiring, onboarding, and training Implemented and managed the pipeline for multiple designers across pods through a successful worldwide launch.
  • Designed several features that produced measurable increase in early retention and conversion
  • Collaborated with engineering to define and create a component based UI framework which reduced UI implementation time by ~30% and improved overall quality and consistency.
  • Implemented and managed the pipeline for multiple designers across pods.
  • Established internal UX processes; contributions include process and pipeline creation for visual design, design, and development
  • Co-authored the company’s UX career track, established the company’s first cross-studio design meetings, and cultivated team culture through collaborative training, presentations, and team events.
